Development of algorithms to control smart cars using computer vision. Through a CCD camera and line scan sensor the track can be detected for subsequent controlling servo motors and CC motors of traction. This project took 3 years and we had built several algorithms for smart cars line followers and track followers. In 2014 one of this smart car won the Freescale Cup Brazil and took us to Germany (Fraunhofer IIS) for Freescale Cup World Finals (2015).
